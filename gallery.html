Welcome to My Afterlife Series Page


Introduction

Welcome to the Afterlife Series, a bold 2D/2.5D animated series that’ll ignite your imagination! Crafted by a 20-year-old visionary in SaskPolytech’s IDT Program, this pulse-pounding adventure follows a cyan colored robot, Jason, as he unravels the secrets of his past in a post-apocalyptic Hawaii, reborn from the ashes of WW3. With lush, radioactive wilderness and vibrant characters, Afterlife blends Hawaiian mythology, futuristic flair, and deep moral questions to captivate modern audiences.

Meet the Heroes:


Jason

Jason, a cyan diamond-shaped robot, is a royal knight with a mysterious past. Wielding a powerful sword, his white-hot eyes blaze when threatened, unleashing lightning-fast slashes. He’s on a quest to uncover his origins, his parents’ fate, and the truth behind his blade.

Michael

Michael, a lime-green spark, is Jason’s loyal best friend and a noble citizen, always ready to explore Maui’s eerie ruins.

Rosaline

Rosaline, a pink-hued princess, is the compassionate heir to the Kingdom of Arcadia, balancing royal duty with her growing bond with Jason.

Meet the Villains:


Malachi, a towering black robot, rules the Badlands with an iron grip. Guided by the sinister Caedes, a fallen leader reborn by evil spirits, Malachi threatens the Kingdom’s fragile peace.
